## Who We Are

HA Consultancy is a boutique Microsoft-focused consultancy based in Dubai, UAE. We design, build, and maintain enterprise solutions across the full Microsoft stack — from deeply customized Dynamics 365 CRM implementations to AI-powered automation and modern SaaS platforms.

We don't just configure software. We architect systems that eliminate manual work, connect disparate platforms, and scale with the businesses we serve.
